# Bio
**Wei-Feng Ou** received the B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Guangdong University of Technology in 2013, the M.E. degree in Signal and Information Processing from South China University of Technology in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from City University of Hong Kong in 2021. He was a Software Engineer at Huawei Technologies from 2016 to 2018, and an Algorithm Researcher at SenseTime from 2021 to 2023. He is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology, Dongguan University of Technology. His research interests include on biometrics, computer vision, and deep learning.
